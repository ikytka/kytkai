## My approach
I decided to document my journey and share it publicly mainly for three reasons: 1) public commitment helps me maintain my motivation and reduces the risk of stopping the next day, 2) perhaps one day someone will find some information mentioned here useful, and 3) it is a way to receive feedback or recommendations from wandering readers on the topics I will be exploring (been there, done that, have you tried...? all these are highly appreciated).

My idea is to write notes with examples I have used. Occasionally, I will reflect on my attempts. As I said, do not expect a detailed plan to be followed. I will do what I feel is working.
