I knew the term FOMO (fear of missing out), but today I learned there is also FOBO (fear of being obsolete). And right after that I came across an article ["GenAI and the psychology of work"](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.tics.2025.04.009) by Erik Hermann, Stefano Puntoni, and Carey K. Morewedge. By quickly skimming the text and thinking how to approach it, I found two terms I was not certain about their meaning. So I asked ChatGPT.

```
what are these two psychological terms: direct resolution, symbolic self- completion ?
```
Good. No I know. But wait, what if I remove the context? So I opened a new chat and tried it.

```
what does it mean: direct resolution, symbolic self- completion ?
```
The answer was also good but I could see the difference that the second result is meant for general public and does not contain references to psychological literature. Nothing new, just never tried to compare results of two similarly defined prompts.
